Skills & Mentality

Everything I build is configered to small mobile screen specifications, then styles are over-ruled with media-queries for a larger screen when required. Aka: Mobile-first.

I limit the use of images and compress them if they are necessary. I favour SVGs and font icons to prevent unnecessary http requests. I don't run any scripts until the DOM is finished loading and I keep everything clean and dry.

I use lightweight css-only responsive grids to ensure my work looks great on any screen size.

I make the browsers job easier by including utilities classes that can be repeated over and over. I currently work to the B.E.M methodology.

I don't claim to be an expert, but I feel I know enough to make sure anything I build performs well in the google pecking-order.
